This section is dedicated to my personal musings.

I owe my life to writing.

My pen has been my best friend ever since I was a child; it never lets me down; never betrays me; knows all of my thoughts, feelings, and secrets; always here for me when I need it, with no judgement and no condescension; gives me the ultimate freedom of expression; never jealous; and always giving until there's nothing left to give; the perfect relationship.
